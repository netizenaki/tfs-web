Admission Guide

Admissions requirements

Built for postgraduate readiness

Pre-Masters programs bridge the gap between undergraduate study and postgraduate entry, combining English language development with subject-specific academic modules. Entry is open to graduates who hold a bachelor's degree but need language or academic bridging support.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent qualification
  • English language results (IELTS/TOEFL)
  • Personal statement and academic goals
  • Official transcripts from undergraduate institution
  • Reference from academic or professional employer
