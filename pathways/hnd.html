Admission Guide

Admissions requirements

Practical, applied learning

HND and NCC programs are designed around applied coursework and real-world skills. Admission typically requires previous academic records, basic English proficiency, and relevant documentation. No highly competitive entry process — the focus is on commitment to hands-on learning.
