Admission Guide

Admissions requirements

Rigorous, balanced academics

The IB Diploma demands strong academic commitment across six subject groups plus the core elements: Theory of Knowledge, Extended Essay, and Creativity, Activity, Service. Entry requires a strong secondary school record and genuine intellectual curiosity.
