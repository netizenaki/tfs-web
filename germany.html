Overview

Germany is a leading federal parliamentary republic in Central Europe with the continent's largest economy. With a population of over 84 million, it is a key European Union member known for engineering, innovation, and a rich history, featuring 16 federal states and a capital in Berlin.

Student Guide

Admissions and living essentials at a glance

Admission Requirements

Most applications combine academic eligibility, language readiness, and clear study intent.

What students usually prepare

  • Academic transcripts and graduation records
  • English or German language proficiency results
  • Statement of purpose and CV
  • Passport copy and supporting documents
  • Program-specific portfolio or subject prerequisites

Cost of Living

Planning a realistic budget early helps students choose the right city and study timeline.

Common expense areas

  • Rent, dormitory, or shared accommodation
  • Food, groceries, and daily transport
  • Health insurance and residence-related fees
  • Study materials and semester contributions
  • Personal spending and seasonal travel
