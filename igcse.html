Admissions requirement

Simple admission requirements

IGCSE admission typically requires students to submit previous academic records, demonstrate basic English language proficiency, and provide supporting documents such as identification papers, transcripts, recommendation letters, or passport copies. Some schools may also conduct placement tests or interviews to evaluate the student’s academic level and readiness.
