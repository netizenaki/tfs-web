About The Forward Society

Our Story of
helping students move forward globally

From its early workshops to a trusted consultancy network, The Forward Society has grown by focusing on student outcomes, transparency, and practical admissions strategy.

Mission and Vision

The principles behind our work

Mission

Accessible global opportunities

Deliver personalized and ethical consultancy that helps students enter programs aligned with long-term outcomes.

Vision

A network of future-ready learners

Build a trusted global community where students confidently pursue academic and professional growth abroad.

Timeline

How we grew over the years

2018

Founded with student-first advising

We began with personalized coaching for students preparing for international university applications.

2020

Expanded pathways and destinations

Our consultancy broadened support across Europe and Asia with specialized admissions plans.

2023

Scaled mentor and webinar network

We launched larger student workshops and introduced dedicated pathway mentors.

2026

Trusted premium advisory experience

Today, students choose us for data-backed decisions and future-focused global education guidance.
