Student Guide

Admissions and living essentials at a glance

A destination for connected learning

Belgium combines research excellence with a deeply international campus culture. Students benefit from strong English-taught programs, affordable tuition compared to other Western European countries, and easy access to the broader EU job market.

Admission Requirements

  • Academic transcripts and graduation records
  • English or French/Dutch language proficiency
  • Statement of purpose and CV
  • Passport copy and supporting documents
  • Program-specific prerequisites or portfolio

Cost of Living

  • Accommodation in student housing or shared apartments
  • Food, transport, and daily expenses
  • Health insurance and registration fees
  • Study materials and university fees
  • Cultural activities and occasional travel
